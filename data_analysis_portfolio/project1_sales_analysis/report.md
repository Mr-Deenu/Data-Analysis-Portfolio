# Retail Sales Analysis Report

## Objective

Understand sales performance, product profitability, and customer behavior using retail transaction data.

---

## Key Findings

* Sales peak during year-end season
* Technology category generates highest profit
* Some sub-categories consistently create losses
* High discounts reduce profit margin
* Few customers contribute majority revenue

---

## Business Insights

* Revenue depends heavily on repeat customers
* Discount strategy negatively affects profitability
* Product mix optimization required

---

## Recommendations

* Introduce loyalty rewards for top customers
* Reduce excessive discounting
* Remove or improve loss-making products
* Focus marketing on high-value customers

---

## Tools Used

Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
