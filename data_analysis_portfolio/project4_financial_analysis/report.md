# Financial Market Analysis Report

## Objective

Analyze stock performance, risk, and portfolio diversification.

---

## Key Findings

* Technology stocks showed highest growth
* Diversified portfolio reduced volatility
* Correlation observed between similar sector stocks
* Market direction difficult to predict accurately

---

## Investment Insight

Diversification improves stability but limits extreme gains.

---

## Recommendations

* Maintain diversified portfolio
* Avoid investing in single high-risk stock
* Focus on long-term investment strategy

---

## Tools Used

Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, yfinance, Scikit-learn
