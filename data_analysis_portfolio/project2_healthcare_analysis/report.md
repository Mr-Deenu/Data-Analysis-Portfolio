# Healthcare Diabetes Prediction Report

## Objective

Predict diabetes risk using patient medical attributes.

---

## Key Findings

* Glucose level strongest indicator of diabetes
* BMI and Age also significant factors
* Model accuracy ~75%

---

## Medical Insight

Model can help early detection of high-risk patients and reduce complications.

---

## Recommendations

* Regular screening for high glucose patients
* Preventive monitoring for high BMI patients
* Use predictive tools in primary healthcare centers

---

## Tools Used

Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Seaborn
