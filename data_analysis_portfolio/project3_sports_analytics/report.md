# Sports Match Winner Prediction Report

## Objective

Predict match winner using team, toss and venue information.

---

## Key Findings

* Match outcomes highly unpredictable
* Toss winner slightly increases win probability
* Venue influences match result
* Some teams perform consistently better

---

## Sports Insight

Cricket performance depends on situational factors, not just team strength.

---

## Recommendations

* Teams should analyze venue history before matches
* Toss strategy should be optimized
* Use data-driven match preparation

---

## Tools Used

Python, Pandas, Random Forest, Seaborn
